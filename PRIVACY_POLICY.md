# Privacy Policy for EduMaster

**Last Updated:** November 17, 2025

## Introduction

EduMaster ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Study Progress Data**: We store your study sessions, flashcard reviews, scores, and progress locally on your device.
- **User Preferences**: Theme settings, notification preferences, and app customization options.
- **Username**: The display name you choose for your profile (stored locally).

### Automatically Collected Information
- **App Usage Data**: Study statistics, streaks, achievements, and learning analytics (stored locally on your device).
- **Device Information**: We do not collect device information or send it to external servers.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide spaced repetition learning functionality
- Track your study progress and statistics
- Calculate achievements and streaks
- Customize your learning experience
- Send study reminder notifications (if enabled)

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not:
- Collect personal information from external servers
- Transmit your data to remote servers
- Share your information with third parties
- Use cloud storage or backup services

Your study data remains entirely on your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else.

## Third-Party Services

EduMaster does NOT integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. Your data is completely private and local.

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your locally stored data:
- Data is stored in a secure SQLite database
- App data is protected by Android's app sandbox
- No internet connection is required for core functionality

## Notifications

If you enable study reminders:
- Notifications are generated locally on your device
- We do not collect information about notification interactions
- You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings

## Children's Privacy

EduMaster is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally on the device, parents have full control over the app's data through device management.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data
- **Modify**: Change your username, preferences, and settings at any time
- **Export**: Your data remains on your device and can be backed up using Android backup features (if enabled by you)

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:
1. Uninstall the EduMaster app from your device
2. All locally stored data will be permanently removed

Alternatively, you can clear app data through your device settings (Settings > Apps > EduMaster > Storage > Clear Data).

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Showing an in-app notice for significant changes

## International Users

EduMaster is available globally. All data processing occurs locally on your device, regardless of your location.

## GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

For users in the European Union:
- **Legal Basis**: We process data based on your consent and to fulfill our contract to provide educational services
- **Data Portability**: Your data is accessible within the app at all times
- **Right to Erasure**: Uninstalling the app deletes all personal data
- **No Profiling**: We do not use automated decision-making or profiling

## CCPA Compliance (California Users)

For California residents:
- We do not sell your personal information
- We do not share your personal information with third parties
- You have the right to delete your information (by uninstalling the app)
